TYPEFACE CLASSIFICATION CARDS

I created Mini cards, both front and back, to demonstrate my love of the different typeface classifications.

AUDREY HEPBURN MAGAZINE SPREADS

Two magazine layouts based on distinct aspects of the life of Audrey Hepburn-someone who has always been a source of inspiration for me.

KAT X SKY

A depiction of multicultural love, silhouetted by touching words of personal evolution written by Skyler (right), standing self-assured as Kat rests on their shoulder.

A FLEETING MOMENT

Using long exposure, I took this shot in Downtown Los Angeles to represent a moment frozen in time; inspired by Henri Cartier Bresson.

MAGAZINE LAYOUT

Using my own photograph (left), I wanted to create a simple and modern magazine layout using Lorem Ipsum words.

THE RESTLESS CITY

I combined two photos I took of Tokyo; one in the morning, one at night.

MAN VS NATURE

Defying the rule of thirds, I was drawn to take this shot because the man is so small and the ocean is so vast, yet they are still valued the same in my eyes.

MAGAZINE COVER

Based on a pre-existing boy's Japanese magazine called Popeye Magazine, I wanted to challenge gender norms; I used photoshop to place myself onto the background image, which I also took, then manually added everything else on after, such as my own sub-headers.
